🔒 Privacy — everything stays on your device

  • The site collects zero personal data. There is no server storing anything — these pages are static files.
  • The name you type and the sound on/off setting are saved only in your browser's local storage, on your device. They are never sent anywhere, and clearing your browser data removes them completely.
  • No cookies, no analytics, no third-party scripts, no social buttons, no external requests of any kind.
  • The spoken words ("A! Apple!") use your device's built-in text-to-speech — no audio is recorded or transmitted.

💡 Tips for a good session

  • Press F11 (fullscreen) so little fingers can't wander into browser tabs. Press it again to exit.
  • System shortcuts (like closing the window) can't be blocked by any website — staying nearby is part of the fun anyway.
  • Short sessions work best for toddlers — 5–10 minutes of play, ideally together with you naming what appears.
  • Works on laptops and desktops with a keyboard; Balloon Pop, Finger Paint and Animal Parade also play great on touchscreens.
