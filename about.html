💛 Our Story

Hi! We're Anshul and Priyanka — two new parents who discovered, like every parent does, that tiny hands are magnetically attracted to keyboards.

Instead of pulling our daughter Prisha away from the laptop, we thought: what if every key she pressed did something wonderful? What if mashing the keyboard taught her letters, colors, numbers, animal sounds, and music?

That's how Prisha's Playground was born — a tiny collection of games where a toddler can't do anything wrong. Every tap, every key, every scribble is rewarded with something delightful.

We built it for Prisha, but we'd love it to help your little one too.

🌱 What we believe

  • Play is learning. Toddlers learn by cause and effect — press, see, hear, giggle, repeat.
  • Kids' attention is not for sale. No ads, ever. No purchases. No "watch a video to continue".
  • Privacy is the default. Nothing your child does here ever leaves your device. Read the details →
  • Simple is better. No installs, no accounts, no updates. Open the link and play.
